ABOUT OUR JOURNY

Two Friends; two dreamers; dreamt in their college life to do something; do something for our education system;for the bangladeshi students.They wanted to make a platform where they could bring the students to a new horizon of their life. One of them got chance in Buet and other was in Dhaka University.The milestone might be founded in the basis of this phenomena.They founded a co-educational platform named "steps" so that they could help the students like them who had enough potential and make them bloom. They made the things easier for the students to get chance in reputed universities.Every year few students got chance in public universities and they also joined at steps to contribute as well.They also became the heroes of new students; became an ideal stepsian. During the pandemic of corona, parvej and rony, two pioneer, again founded this online platform named "stepsians" with the stepsians to support all the students from home and the endless journey started, and obviously it is free.
